NANAIMO DIAMONDS

ABOUT US

"The Nanaimo Diamonds Synchorized Swim Club fosters a positive environment where swimmers of all abilities have the opportunity to reach their potential."

Club Goals

  • To provide a high standard of coaching and training for all swimmers
  • To provide an opportunity to achieve excellence through competition, good sportsmanship and physical fitness.
  • To provide recreational introduction to synchronized swimming
  • To provide opportunity for fun, camaraderie and travel.
  • To provide a program that best suits swimmers individual needs and abilities, in order to help them achieve their goals.

History of Our Club

The Nanaimo Diamonds Synchro Club was established in 1984 by a group of passionate parents and dedicated swimmers who followed through with their vision, and here we are today. The governing philosophy of the Club is to provide the best in training opportunities for our swimmers.

Our club is registered under the BC Societies Act. This status allows us to operate as a non-profit organization. We are members of the Canadian Amateur Synchronized Swimming Association and of Synchro BC.

In the past ten years our club has almost tripled its numbers sitting at just over 50 athletes, from our Aqua Squirts Programs which include swimmers as young as three years old to our Masters Programs which have fifty year young athletes!
