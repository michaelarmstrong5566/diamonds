PROVINCIAL STREAM

Ages: 7+ yrs old (or coach recommendation)

Pre-Requisite: Comfortable in deep water unassisted, A desire to compete in the sport, Commitment to practices & competitions

For ages 8 and over. Swimmers practice 3-6 days per week, and have approx 7+ hours of pool time per week. A high level of commitment is required at these levels. Travel is required for competition in province and Nationalily out of province. There is approx 4-6 competitions per season. National and Provincial level programs. Swimmers will perform in two club performances per season as well.
