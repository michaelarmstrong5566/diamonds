Development Program

At the development level, we take a great deal of time to learn the basics and try to learn the basics and try to integrate stroke development and other fundamentals into the realm of creativity and choreography. Stroke development and basic synchro skills (scullling, head checks, pool ... Participation and attendance is very important. The girls are part of a team so everyone needs to join in the chroeography development and memorization! Dryland training, is also part of our teachings which consists of strength training, flexibility, and land drill (this is what we call it when we practice routines on land).

Most of all we recognize the importance of social development with girls this age so encouraging them to do their best and focus on their strength is a priority in the Development Program! We have the luxury of swimming at the sam etime as the competitive swimmers so our girls can see what higher leveled swimmers do. What amazing role models for them! If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contract the coaches.

Aqua Squirts

For ages 3-8 years old, screening is provided. Swimmers swim 1 hour per week. Swimmers will work on stroke development, creative movement, an introduction to synchro basics and routine development. Swimmers will perform in two club performances per season. Registration is ongoing throughout season.

Masters Recreation & Competitive Programs

Ages 17 and over. Programs are based on numbers and levels of athletes. Options to compete at each level. Swimmers will perform in two club performances per season. As well as the possibility of recreational and provincial level meets.
